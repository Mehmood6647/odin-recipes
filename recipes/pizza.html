Classic Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a Classic Pepperoni Pizza, It is an Iconic Italian dish that is very simple to make. It's simply a bread (soft on the inside and crispy on the outside) and topped whatever sauce you like, topped with your favorite vegetables and meats.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Pepperoni
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix 1 tbsp of yeast with 1/2 cup of water and dissolve it and set aside for 15 mins.
  2. Now we mix the flour with water and then add your yeast mixture and combine these ingredients well until it becomes a dough.
  3. Now set aside your dough for 30-60 mins to rise.
  4. Now take out the dough and roll it until it's thin and round.
  5. Now apply Marinara sauce on the dough and add your Pepperoni's and Cheese.
  6. Bake it for 15-25 mins or until the bottom looks cooked.
  7. Let it rest a few minutes and Enjoy!
