Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a classic breakfast and they are very easy to make and are very Delicious.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Baking Powder
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Maple Syrup

Steps

  1. Mix flour with milk, baking soda, eggs, pinch of salt and some sugar.
  2. Mix these ingredients and make a thick but spreadable batter.
  3. Keep the pan heat on Medium-Low and Put the batter in.
  4. Cook the batter 1-1/2 mins each side.
  5. Put the butter on pancakes and pour maple syruo on as much you desire and Enjoy!
