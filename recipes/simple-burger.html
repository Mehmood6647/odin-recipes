Simple Burger

Description

This is a recipe for all the people who want a quick, simple and delicious meal. And the best part is that we don't have to prepare anything bcz every item in this recipe will be store bought.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Burgers
  • Burger Buns
  • Condaments
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook our frozen burgers in a skillet for 2-3 mins per side.
  2. Take out the patties and immediately put a slice of cheese on top.
  3. Toast our buns in the same skillet
  4. Apply your favorite condaments on the buns
  5. Put everything together and Enjoy!
